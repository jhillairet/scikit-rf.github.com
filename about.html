About¶

scikit-rf was born in the University of Virginia’s millimeter wave research lab in 2009, to provide functionality that was not available commercially. It was originally called mwavepy, but was re-branded a scikit to emphasize its relationship to the scientific python community.

It is currently being developed by a Alex Arsenovic, and others on Github .
